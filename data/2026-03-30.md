### [1] [Particle Acceleration in Cassiopeia A Revealed by Broadband High-Energy Spectrum](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.26326)
*Bo-Tao Li,Wei Wang,Zhuo Li*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 该论文重新分析超新星遗迹仙后座A的伽马射线谱，发现其双峰谱可由壳层质子-质子碰撞和逆康普顿散射解释，而喷流同步辐射可解释硬X射线，喷流质子加速能量可达5×10⁴⁷ erg，暗示此类超新星遗迹可能是银河系PeV宇宙线源。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Fermi-LAT和LHAASO更新了仙后座A的GeV-sub-PeV能谱观测数据，需要重新评估这个年轻超新星遗迹的粒子加速能力，特别是其作为银河系PeV宇宙线源（PeVatron）的可能性。

Method: 使用先前建立的壳层+喷流不对称模型，通过宽带谱拟合分析伽马射线谱。壳层部分考虑质子-质子碰撞和逆康普顿散射，喷流部分考虑同步辐射和质子-质子碰撞，并与LHAASO-KM2A的观测限制进行比较。

Result: 双峰伽马射线谱可由壳层质子-质子碰撞和逆康普顿散射很好解释；喷流同步辐射能解释高达220 keV的硬X射线；喷流质子加速能量可达5×10⁴⁷ erg，若假设PeV宇宙线在银河系呈团块分布且团块大小与银盘厚度相当，则计算出的质子通量与1 PeV处观测值一致。

Conclusion: 仙后座A类超新星遗迹仍可能是银河系PeV宇宙线源，这鼓励LHAASO和未来望远镜更精确地探测或限制100 TeV以上的谱线。

Abstract: Recently, the GeV--sub-PeV spectrum of supernova remnant (SNR) Cassiopeia A (Cas A), one of the youngest and most well-studied SNRs in our Galaxy, has been updated by observations of Fermi-LAT and LHAASO. We revisit Cas A with our previous shell-plus-jet asymmetric model and investigate its particle acceleration ability. The broadband fitting results suggest that the double-peaked gamma-ray spectrum can be well attributed to proton-proton (PP) collisions and inverse Compton scattering within the SNR shell, while the synchrotron emission from a jet component with velocity of $\sim0.1c$ can account for the hard X-ray emission up to 220 keV. Furthermore, the PP collisions in the jet can produce a sub-PeV emission, but constrained by the LHAASO-KM2A limit to a flux below $\sim 1\times10^{-14}\rm erg/(cm^2s)$ at 100 TeV. The energy of accelerated protons in the jet of Cas A could be up to $5\times10^{47}$ erg, which, assuming that the PeV cosmic ray distribution is clumpy in the Galaxy with the clump size comparable to the thickness of the Galactic plane, derives a proton flux consistent with the observed one at 1 PeV, implying that the Cas A-like SNRs can still be PeVatrons in the Galaxy. It is encouraging for LHAASO and future telescopes to detect or constrain Cas A spectrum above 100-TeV more precisely.

### [2] [The Gravitational-Wave Power Gap in Core-Collapse Supernovae: Insights from 60 Axisymmetric Simulations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.26408)
*Haakon Andresen,Xingzao Li,Aurore Betranhandy,Evan P. O'Connor,Shuai Zha,Sean M. Couch*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 该研究分析了60个二维核心坍缩超新星模拟的引力波发射，重点关注频谱中发射功率被强烈抑制的窄频区间（功率间隙），发现间隙频率与中子星内核心物理性质存在强相关性，揭示了间隙可能源于窄带振荡模式与宽带背景信号的相消干涉。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究旨在理解核心坍缩超新星引力波频谱中出现的功率间隙现象，探究其物理起源以及与中子星内核心性质的关系，从而为通过引力波观测获取极端密度物质行为信息提供理论基础。

Method: 对60个二维核心坍缩超新星模拟进行引力波发射分析，覆盖不同前身星模型和状态方程，重点研究功率间隙频率与模拟物理性质的相关性，并检验文献中提出的多种机制解释，最后探索窄带振荡模式与宽带背景信号相消干涉的假设场景。

Result: 发现功率间隙频率与形成中子星内核心的性质（如声速）存在强且系统的相关性，表明间隙编码了极端密度物质行为的信息；验证了文献中提出的机制解释；证明窄带振荡模式与宽带背景信号的相消干涉能够在发射功率中产生尖锐的最小值。

Conclusion: 引力波频谱中的功率间隙频率与中子星内核心物理性质密切相关，可能源于窄带振荡模式与宽带背景信号的相消干涉，这为通过引力波观测探测极端密度物质行为提供了新的可能性。

Abstract: We analyse the gravitational-wave emission from 60 two-dimensional core-collapse supernova simulations. The models cover a range of progenitors and equations of state. We focus on the narrow frequency interval in the gravitational-wave spectrum where the emitted power is strongly suppressed (the power gap) and how its central frequency relates to the physical properties of the simulations. We find that the power-gap frequency exhibits strong and systematic correlations with the properties of the inner core of the forming neutron star, for example the sound speed, suggesting that the gap encodes information about the behaviour of matter at extreme densities. We further examine how well several mechanisms proposed in the literature account for the presence and evolution of the gap in our simulations. Finally, we explore a scenario in which the gap arises from destructive interference between a narrow oscillation mode and a broadband background signal, demonstrating that such an interaction can produce a sharp minimum in the emitted gravitational-wave power.

### [3] [Recoil geometry determines electromagnetic counterparts from supermassive black hole merger remnants](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.26641)
*Yoonsoo Kim,Elias R. Most,Hai-Yang Wang*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 首次进行反冲黑洞与磁滞吸积盘相互作用的广义相对论磁流体动力学模拟，揭示了不同反冲几何结构导致的不同喷流和吸积盘行为，为多信使观测提供理论依据。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究气体环境中合并的双黑洞（如超大质量黑洞双星）在引力辐射反冲后与周围吸积盘的相互作用，探索其独特的后合并电磁对应体，为即将到来的空间引力波探测时代提供多信使观测理论基础。

Method: 首次进行广义相对论磁流体动力学模拟，追踪反冲黑洞与磁滞吸积盘的相互作用，并一致跟踪从螺旋阶段到后合并的演化过程。

Result: 不同反冲几何结构导致截然不同的喷流和吸积盘行为：垂直反冲时内盘保持引力束缚并维持相对论喷流；面内反冲导致大量冲击加热和可能的喷流淬灭；倾斜反冲则因吸积盘倾斜产生间歇性爆发。

Conclusion: 这些电磁对应体的多波段监测，结合同时的引力波探测，将有助于表征合并环境的物理条件，为多信使天文学提供重要工具。

Abstract: Merging binary black holes embedded in gaseous environments, such as supermassive black hole binaries following gas-rich galaxy mergers, are promising sources of multi-messenger transients in the upcoming age of space-based gravitational wave detections. In case a gravitational radiation recoil is imparted to the merger remnant, subsequent interactions between the recoiled black hole and its circumbinary disk may lead to unique post-merger electromagnetic counterparts. We present the first general relativistic magnetohydrodynamic simulations of a recoiling black hole interacting with a magnetically arrested circumbinary disk the evolution of which has been consistently tracked through the inspiral phase. We show that the post-merger accretion dynamics, depending on the recoil geometry, exhibits qualitatively disparate jet and disk behavior. For recoils perpendicular to the disk, the inner disk remains gravitationally bound and sustains relativistic jets, while in-plane recoils lead to copious shock heating and potential jet quenching for black holes directly colliding with the disk. Oblique recoils, on the other hand, produce intermittent outbursts from jet-disk interactions owing to the tilt introduced in the accretion disk. Multi-wavelength monitoring of these electromagnetic counterparts, in conjunction with the coincident gravitational wave detection, will be able to aid in characterizing the physical conditions of the merger environment.

</details>


### [4] [Gaia Sees Blazars Move: Locating Optical Flares Using Astrometry](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.26662)
*Alexander Plavin*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 通过结合Gaia DR3自行运动和时变测光，发现耀变体在耀发时光学位置会移动，且上升流量驱动质心向上游移动，下降流量驱动质心向下游移动，可用简单的双成分模型解释。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究耀变体在光学耀发期间的位置变化，以几何方法定位光学耀发的位置，为更高能耀发的空间定位提供独立的空间锚点。

Method: 结合Gaia DR3自行运动和时变测光数据，分析74个具有显著自行运动的耀变体，使用简单的双成分模型（恒定扩展发射+耀发区域）解释观测模式。

Result: 在74个源中，60个显示出流量上升时质心向上游移动、流量下降时向下游移动的普遍模式。通过几何方法将光学耀发定位到VLBI位置的<1 mas范围内（典型耀变体距离下约几秒差距），位于最内层喷流或吸积盘中。

Conclusion: 这种纯几何方法无需多波段相关或模型依赖假设，为定位更高能耀发提供了独立的空间锚点。未来的Gaia DR4每历元天体测量将进一步收紧约束。

Abstract: When blazars flare, their optical position moves. We show this by combining Gaia DR3 proper motions with epoch photometry for blazars with strong optical jet emission. In 60 of 74 sources with significant proper motion, rising flux drives the centroid upstream while fading flux drives it downstream - a near-universal pattern captured by a simple two-component model of constant extended emission and a flaring region. Using this connection, we geometrically localize the optical flares to within <1 mas of the VLBI position - a few parsecs at typical blazar distances - placing them in the innermost jet or accretion disk. This purely geometric method requires no multi-wavelength correlations or model-dependent assumptions, and provides an independent spatial anchor for localizing higher-energy flares. Per-epoch astrometry from Gaia DR4 is set to tighten our constraints even further.

</details>

TL;DR: 本文报告了LST-1望远镜在2023年12月首次探测到FSRQ OP 313的甚高能γ射线耀发，这是目前观测到的最远VHE耀变体（z=0.997）。通过多波段数据分析，研究团队表征了耀发与平静态的γ射线发射，利用其远距离约束河外背景光（EBL）衰减，并采用双区轻子模型构建了宽带能谱分布（SED），发现多个外部光子场（宽线区、吸积盘、尘埃环）均可解释观测结果，但主导机制仍不明确。

Motivation: 探测并表征最远耀变体OP 313（z=0.997）的甚高能γ射线耀发，利用其极远距离研究河外背景光（EBL）对VHE光子的衰减效应，并理解此类高红移源中γ射线产生的物理机制。

Method: 利用LST-1和MAGIC的VHE数据、Fermi-LAT的HE数据及准同时多波段观测，系统探索EBL强度约束γ射线谱衰减，并采用双区轻子模型（含吸积盘、尘埃环、宽线区热成分）构建宽带SED。

Result: HE波段耀发亮度比Fermi-LAT平均态高出约50倍；HE与VHE联合观测对EBL密度设定了新约束；双区轻子模型成功拟合SED，但多个外部光子场（宽线区、吸积盘、尘埃环）组合均可解释观测，主导机制未确定。

Conclusion: 成功探测到迄今最远VHE耀变体的γ射线耀发，展示了新一代望远镜在红移~1处观测VHE源的能力；对EBL的约束提升了宇宙背景光模型认知；但耀发期间主导外部光子场仍需进一步多波段观测来区分。

Abstract: In December 2023, the Large-Sized Telescope prototype (LST-1) detected for the first time VHE $γ$-ray emission from the FSRQ OP~313 becoming the furthest blazar ever observed at VHE with z=0.997. We aim to characterize the $γ$-ray emission of OP 313 during this flare, comparing it with its average emission state in order to understand the processes leading to this detection. Its remarkable distance also enables studies on the Extragalactic Background Light (EBL), with the goal of evaluating the attenuation of VHE $γ$-ray photons. We characterize the $γ$-ray emission during the flare in December 2023 and the low emission state observed in January 2024 thanks to the LST-1 and MAGIC data and quasi-simultaneous \textit{Fermi}-LAT observations. This dataset also enables us to evaluate the EBL attenuation by systematically exploring the EBL intensity over the $γ$-ray spectrum. Finally, we study the multi-wavelength emission and interpret the broadband spectral energy distribution (SED) within blazar radiative models including the thermal contributions from the accretion disc, dusty torus and broad line region. We also characterize the flare brightness in the high-energy (HE, $E>100$~MeV) $γ$-ray band, that was found to be a factor 50 above the average emission seen by \textit{Fermi}-LAT. The HE and VHE observations allow us to set constrains to the EBL density. Finally, thanks to the extensive multi-wavelength campaign organized, we are able to construct and model the broadband SED of OP~313 within the framework of a two-zone leptonic model where the $γ$-ray emission is produced via inverse-Compton scattering of the broad line region, accretion disk and dusty torus photon fields. However the dominant external photon field remains unknown, as several combinations are able to successfully explain the $γ$-ray emission observed.

### [2] [Supermassive Black Holes: modelling strongly and weakly magnetised misaligned accretion disks](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.27334)
*Joshua Samuel Stanway,Cora Prather,Derek Ward-Thompson,Timothy J. Walton,Brett Patterson,Hyerin Cho*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 本文通过GRMHD模拟研究发现，磁囚禁盘(MAD)状态下的吸积盘即使初始倾角高达75°，也能在距黑洞约10倍史瓦西半径内与黑洞自转轴对齐，而传统SANE状态盘则保持未对齐状态，且最大盘倾角存在约50°的上限。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探究非对齐吸积盘在不同磁场配置下围绕旋转超大质量黑洞的演化行为，特别是磁场强度对盘-黑洞对齐过程的影响。

Method: 使用广义相对论磁流体动力学(GRMHD)代码KHARMA，对初始盘倾角15°、45°、75°结合MAD和SANE两种磁场配置进行参数化数值模拟研究。

Result: MAD模型在极端倾角(75°)下也能在~10 r_g范围内与黑洞对齐；SANE模型始终保持未对齐，最大盘倾角约50°且与初始倾角呈非线性关系；KHARMA模拟结果与其他GRMHD代码一致。

Conclusion: 动态重要的磁场（MAD状态）显著促进吸积盘与黑洞的对齐过程，而弱磁场（SANE状态）下的盘对齐程度有限，且盘倾角存在饱和值。

Abstract: In this paper, we carry out a numerical study of misaligned accretion disks around spinning supermassive black holes. Here, we conduct a parameter survey covering a range of initial disk misalignment angles ($\mathcal{T}_\mathrm{init}=15^\circ, 45^\circ, 75^\circ$) with either the Magnetically Arrested Disk (MAD) or Standard And Normal Evolution (SANE) magnetic field configurations, using the general-relativistic magnetohydrodynamic (GRMHD) code KHARMA. We find that models in the MAD state can align with the black hole up to $\sim10 \, r_g$, even in extremely misaligned models ($\mathcal{T}_\mathrm{init}=75^\circ$), which has not been seen before. Models without a dynamically important magnetic field remain misaligned up to the black hole, with a maximum disk tilt at $\sim10 \, r_g$, the peak's radial distance from the black hole increases with increasing disk misalignment. However, the maximum disk tilt does not have a linear relationship with the initial disk misalignment, and appears to have a maximum value of $\sim50^\circ$. We also show misaligned disk simulations produced in KHARMA are consistent with other GRMHD codes, for a variety of problems.

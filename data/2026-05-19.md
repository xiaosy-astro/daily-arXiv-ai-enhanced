### [1] [Spectral-Regime Overlap and Transition-like Behavior in the Blazar Population from Multi-Instrument X-ray and TeV Observations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.15867)
*Javaid Tantry,Naseer Iqbal*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 本文通过多波段观测数据（Swift-XRT、NuSTAR、XMM-Newton等）分析耀变体（blazar）的X射线和TeV光子指数分布，发现EHBL、HBL、IBL、LBL和FSRQ等子类之间存在广泛的谱指数重叠区域（中间态集中在Γ_X ≈ 2，范围1.5–2.2），并观测到显著的源内谱演化（如Mrk 421的随机变化、OJ 287的状态跃迁），支持耀变体子类并非严格分立，而是由喷流发射和辐射过程长期演化驱动的连续、重叠光谱种群。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统上将耀变体分为BL Lac天体和平谱射电类星体（FSRQs），分别对应辐射低效和高效的吸积模式。然而越来越多证据表明这种二分法并不严格，存在许多中间或过渡态源。光子指数能追踪非热发射谱的演化，是研究喷流能量学、粒子加速和辐射过程的有效探针，因此有必要系统研究耀变体各子类的光子指数分布和长期演化。

Method: 利用Swift-XRT、Swift-BAT、NuSTAR、ROSAT、Chandra、XMM-Newton、NICER、AstroSat、TeVcat以及档案VizieR编译等多任务观测数据，分析多个耀变体子类的X射线和TeV光子指数的分布及长期演化特征。

Result: 1) 发现EHBL、HBL、IBL、LBL和FSRQ-like种群在光子指数空间存在广泛的重叠区域，呈现连续而非截然分离的谱分布；2) 中间态集中在Γ_X ≈ 2，范围Γ_X ~ 1.5–2.2；3) 多历元观测显示显著的源内谱演化，包括Mrk 421的随机变化和OJ 287的状态依赖跃迁；4) 多个源谱指数变化ΔΓ_X > 0.5，中间态占据率约22%–43%（依仪器而异）；5) 多个天体反复穿越传统上区分不同子类的谱区，支持其作为候选过渡态耀变体的解释。

Conclusion: 研究结果倾向于支持一个框架：耀变体子类代表由喷流发射和辐射过程长期演化驱动的重叠且不断演化的光谱种群，而非严格分立类型。

Abstract: Blazars are conventionally classified into BL Lac objects and flat-spectrum radio quasars (FSRQs), commonly associated with radiatively inefficient and efficient accretion onto supermassive black holes, respectively. Increasing evidence, however, suggests that this division is not strictly discrete, with several sources occupying intermediate or transition-like spectral states. Since the photon index traces the evolution of the non-thermal emission spectrum, it provides a useful probe of jet energetics, particle acceleration, and radiative processes across blazar populations. Using multi-mission observations from Swift-XRT, Swift-BAT, NuSTAR, ROSAT, Chandra, XMM-Newton, NICER, AstroSat, TeVcat, and archival VizieR compilations, we investigate the distribution and long-term evolution of X-ray and TeV photon indices across multiple blazar subclasses.
  We identify broad overlap regions in photon-index space linking EHBL, HBL, IBL, LBL, and FSRQ-like populations, suggesting a continuous rather than sharply separated spectral distribution. Across X-ray instruments, the intermediate regime is concentrated near $Γ_{\mathrm{X}} \approx 2$, typically spanning $Γ_{\mathrm{X}} \sim 1.5$--$2.2$. Multi-epoch observations reveal substantial intra-source spectral evolution, including stochastic variability in Mrk~421 and state-dependent transitions in OJ~287. Several sources exhibit spectral-index changes of $ΔΓ_{\mathrm{X}} > 0.5$, while occupancy of the intermediate regime reaches $\sim22$--$43%$ depending on instrument. Multiple objects repeatedly traverse spectral regions connecting traditionally distinct subclasses, supporting their interpretation as candidate transition-like blazars. Overall, the results favor a framework in which blazar subclasses represent overlapping and evolving spectral populations driven by long-term changes in jet emission and radiative processes.

### [2] [Bridging X-ray Polarization with Timing & Spectroscopic Parameters of a galactic black hole: Swift J1727.8-1613](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.15940)
*Arka Chatterjee,Sujoy K. Nath,Kaushik Chatterjee,Samar Safi-Harb,Broja G. Dutta,Indranil Chattopadhyay,Sudip K. Garain,Hsiang-Kuang Chang*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 发现Swift J1727.8-1613在2023年爆发期间，其C型准周期振荡的能量依赖时间延迟与偏振度之间存在显著相关性（斯皮尔曼相关系数0.8），且该相关性与QPO的质量因子有关。通过同时的Insight/HXMT数据分析了光谱特性，并提出逆康普顿散射之外的过程也对偏振和时间延迟有贡献。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探究黑洞双星硬态下光谱、时变和偏振特性之间的关联机制，寻找除逆康普顿化之外的其他物理过程。

Method: 利用IXPE测量能量分辨偏振度和C型QPO的时间延迟，通过Insight/HXMT同步数据进行光谱分析，计算斯皮尔曼相关系数并考察Q因子对相关性的影响。

Result: 能量依赖时间延迟与偏振度之间的斯皮尔曼相关系数为0.8（零假设概率4.2%），且相关性随QPO频率的Q值降低而下降。

Conclusion: 逆康普顿化之外的过程对偏振和时间延迟均有贡献，该相关性可能揭示了黑洞双星硬态下光谱、时变和偏振特性之间更多联系的新机制。

Abstract: We report the discovery of a correlated energy-dependent time lag and degree of polarization for Swift J1727.8-1613 during its 2023 outburst. The energy-dependent time lag is measured around the type-C quasi-periodic oscillations (QPO) observed by IXPE on 2023-09-07, while the degree of polarization is obtained from energy-resolved polarimetric measurements. The Spearman correlation coefficient was found to be 0.8, with a null hypothesis probability of 4.2\%. Furthermore, the correlation value drops as the quality factor, or Q value, of the observed QPO frequencies decreases. The spectral properties of Swift J1727.8-1613 are analyzed using simultaneous Insight/HXMT data. Thereafter, we present model-independent theoretical arguments to show that processes other than inverse Comptonization also contributes to both the observed polarization and time lags. This correlation may therefore point to additional mechanisms contributing to the connection between the spectral, temporal, and polarimetric properties of black hole binaries in their hard state.

### [3] [X-Ray Polarization from the Gamma-Ray Binary LS I +61 303](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.15972)
*Philip Kaaret,Sudip Chakraborty,Daniel Golonka,Oliver J. Roberts,Ioannis Liodakis,Andrea Gnarini,Steven R. Ehlert,Joel B. Coley*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 该研究利用IXPE望远镜对伽马射线双星LS I +61 303进行X射线偏振观测，在2-8 keV波段以4.2σ置信度探测到平均偏振度13.1%的偏振信号。这是第二次在伽马射线双星中探测到X射线同步辐射偏振，表明粒子加速区域的磁场具有显著的有序成分。由于该双星系统的轨道运动测定不精确，导致对X射线偏振角（EVPA）的解释存在歧义。采用不同方法确定的轨道根数会给出不同的EVPA与致密天体-大质量星轴线的夹角关系，其中基于keV/TeV光变曲线建模得出的轨道根数显示两者对齐，这与另一个伽马射线双星PSR B1259-63的观测结果一致。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 通过对伽马射线双星LS I +61 303的X射线偏振观测，揭示其粒子加速区域的磁场结构（有序vs无序），这是理解高能粒子加速机制和双星系统射电-X射线-伽马射线辐射过程的关键。

Method: 利用IXPE望远镜在两个连续轨道周期内（轨道相位0.74-1.05）对LS I +61 303进行成像X射线偏振测量，在2-8 keV波段进行背景扣除后提取偏振信号；并对比四种不同方法（径向速度+射电成像、光学偏振、keV/TeV光变曲线建模）确定的轨道根数，分析X射线EVPA与系统几何的关系。

Result: 在2-8 keV波段以4.2σ显著性探测到平均偏振度13.1%±3.0%的X射线偏振信号，这是第二次在伽马射线双星中探测到X射线同步辐射偏振。不同轨道根数给出不同结论：径向速度+射电成像方法显示EVPA与双星轴线存在约30°偏移；光学偏振方法显示无偏移或垂直取向但要求异常高的轨道倾角；keV/TeV光变曲线建模方法显示EVPA与双星轴线良好对齐。

Conclusion: X射线偏振探测表明LS I +61 303粒子加速区存在有序磁场成分；如果该系统的物理情况与已知的伽马射线双星PSR B1259-63一致，那么基于keV/TeV光变曲线建模得出的轨道根数更可能正确，即X射线EVPA与致密天体-大质量星轴线方向基本一致。

Abstract: The gamma-ray emitting binary stellar system LS I +61 303 was observed with the Imaging X-ray Polarimetry Explorer (IXPE) on two successive orbits over orbital phases of 0.74 to 1.05. Polarization is detected at a significance of 4.2$σ$ with an average polarization degree of $13.1\% \pm 3.0\%$ in the 2-8~keV band after background subtraction. This is the second detection of polarization of the X-ray synchrotron emission from a gamma-ray binary and, again, suggests that the magnetic field in the particle acceleration region has a significant ordered component. The orbital motion on the sky of LS I +61 303 is not well determined, which leads to ambiguity in interpretation of the X-ray electric vector polarization angle (EVPA) measurement. Use of orbital elements determined via radial velocity measurements combined with radio imaging of variable nebular emission, suggests an offset between the X-ray EVPA and the compact object-massive star axis on the order of ~30$^{\circ}$. Such an offset could be produced by Coriolis forces due to binary motion. Use of two different sets orbital elements determined via optical polarimetry suggest either no offset or a perpendicular orientation, but require an unexpectedly high inclination. Use of orbital elements derived from modeling of the keV/TeV light curves suggest good alignment between the X-ray EVPA and the compact object-massive star axis. Such alignment was found for the gamma-ray binary PSR B1259-63. If the same physical situation holds for LS I +61 303, that would favor the orbital elements derived from the keV/TeV light curves.

### [4] [Nucleosynthesis in the fast ejecta of a neutron star merger](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.16132)
*Lukas Schnabel,Stephan Rosswog,Friedrich-Karl Thielemann,Moritz Reichert*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 本文研究了中子星并合中快速抛射物（速度≥0.6c）的核合成过程，发现存在三种核合成通道，其中一种通道会残留大量自由中子。当自由中子质量分数超过0.05时，其β⁻衰变在100-10⁴秒内主导核加热，产生明亮的千新星前驱体（蓝光前兆），ULTRASAT可观测至约200 Mpc。不对称并合或中子星-黑洞并合等产生大潮汐抛射物的系统，尤其可能产生这类明亮蓝光前兆。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 以往研究认为中子星并合抛射物中，少量物质可达相对论速度（≥0.6c），其中中子可能避免被俘获，其衰变可产生早期蓝光前兆。但具体核合成过程及自由中子存活条件尚未被详细研究。

Method: 采用核网络计算，沿参数化轨迹和数值相对论轨迹，详细模拟快速抛射物中的核合成过程，并建立（半）解析模型预测自由中子存活。

Result: 核合成分为三种通道，其中一种通道在主r-process冻结后仍有大量自由中子存活。自由中子质量分数>0.05时，其β⁻衰变在100-10⁴秒主导核加热，产生显著千新星前驱体，ULTRASAT可观测至~200 Mpc。

Conclusion: 不对称中子星并合或中子星-黑洞并合等产生大潮汐抛射物的系统，因低电子分数下中子可存活，可能产生特别明亮的蓝光前兆。

Abstract: Neutron star mergers are today considered a major production site for rapid neutron capture elements. While the bulk of the matter escapes at fast, but non-relativistic velocities (${\sim} 0.2\,c$), a small amount of the dynamically ejected mass reaches mildly relativistic velocities (${\gtrsim}0.6\,c$). It has been suggested earlier, that in such ejecta parts neutrons may avoid being captured and that their decay could power an early blue precursor to the main kilonova event. Here we study in detail the nucleosynthesis in such fast ejecta with nuclear network calculations along both parametrized and numerical relativity trajectories. We find that the nucleosynthesis can be divided into three channels, in one of which a substantial amount of free neutrons survives when the main r-process has frozen out. We provide a (semi-)analytical model for surviving free neutrons which agrees very well with the network calculations. If the mass fraction of the free neutrons exceeds ${\sim} 0.05$, their $β^-$-decay dominates the nuclear heating rate between ${\sim} 100$ and ${\sim} 10^4$ seconds. This dominance leads to a pronounced kilonova precursor that should for plausible ejecta parameters be visible for ULTRASAT out to ${\sim}200\,\rm Mpc$. Since at low electron fractions free neutrons can survive even for moderate velocities, mergers with large tidal ejecta, such as asymmetric neutron star mergers or favorable neutron star black hole mergers, may produce particularly bright blue precursors to their subsequent kilonovae.

</details>

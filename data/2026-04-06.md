### [1] [A high-resolution study of the double radio relic system in MACS J1752.0+4440](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02410)
*M. Della Chiesa,A. Botteon,A. Bonafede,K. Rajpurohit,V. Cuciti,D. Hoang,R. J. van Weeren,X. Zhang,F. Gastaldello*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 研究MACS J1752星系团中的双射电遗迹，通过多波段观测发现其具有平坦的谱指数和复杂的子结构，表明加速机制可能涉及多个激波面、再加速和投影效应，而非简单的单激波模型。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 射电遗迹是位于合并星系团外围的弥散同步辐射源，其起源与星系团内介质中的激波有关，但具体的加速机制仍存在争议。通过研究MACS J1752中的双遗迹系统，可以深入了解射电遗迹的加速机制。

Method: 使用uGMRT、JVLA和LOFAR进行新的宽波段射电连续谱观测，对MACS J1752中的双遗迹系统进行高分辨率谱分析，观察并表征子结构，特别是较亮的遗迹。进行谱曲率分析，生成颜色-颜色图和谱曲率图。

Result: 发现NE遗迹具有双峰表面亮度和谱指数分布，并识别出"亮条"子结构。两个遗迹的积分谱指数异常平坦（α_int^NE = -0.91±0.06，α_int^SW = -0.83±0.05）。谱指数空间变化与观测到的子结构一致。估计注入马赫数为M_NE = 3.1±0.1和M_SW = 3.2±0.1。谱曲率分析显示两个遗迹都有"凹"谱（正谱曲率），与粒子群老化模型不符。

Conclusion: NE遗迹的观测特性与简单的单激波前沿模型不一致。多个激波面、再加速和投影效应可能在塑造遗迹形态中发挥作用。观测到的平坦谱指数和正谱曲率表明加速机制比传统模型更复杂。

Abstract: Radio relics are diffuse, extended synchrotron sources located at the outskirts of merging galaxy clusters. Their origin has been linked with shock waves injected into the intracluster medium, but the acceleration mechanism at the shock front is still under debate. Some clusters, like MACS J1752.0+4440, host a double relic system, with two relics found on opposite sides with respect to the cluster center. To investigate the acceleration mechanism that generates radio relics, we study the morphological and spectral properties of the double relic system in MACS J1752. We present new wideband radio continuum observations made with uGMRT and JVLA, and LOFAR data. We perform a detailed, high-resolution spectral analysis of the double relic system in MACS J1752, observing and characterizing substructures, particularly for the brighter relic. We find a double-peaked surface brightness and spectral index profile for the NE relic and identify a "bright bar" substructure. Moreover, we observed surprisingly flat integrated spectral indices for both relics, at $α_{\mathrm{int}}^{\mathrm{NE}} = -0.91 \pm 0.06$ and $α_{\mathrm{int}}^{\mathrm{SW}} = -0.83 \pm 0.05$. We study the spatial variation of the spectral index, observing a coherent trend with the observed substructures. We estimate an injection Mach number of $\mathcal{M}_{\mathrm{NE}} = 3.1^{+0.1}_{-0.1}$ and $\mathcal{M}_{\mathrm{SW}} = 3.2^{+0.1}_{-0.1}$. By performing a spectral curvature analysis for both relics, generating color-color plots and a spectral curvature maps, we observe two "concave" spectra represented by positive spectral curvature, in contrast with particle population ageing models. The observed properties of the NE relic are not consistent with a simple scenario with a single shock front. Multiple shock surfaces, re-acceleration, and projection effects likely play a role in shaping the morphology of the relic.

### [2] [Pulsar scintillation studies with LOFAR III. Annual variations in PSR~J0814$+$7429](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02681)
*Yanqing Cai,Ziwei Wu,Weiwei Zhu,Joris P. W. Verbiest,Yulan Liu,Krishnakumar Moochickal Ambalappat,Marcus Brüggen,Benedetta Ciardi,Ralf-Jürgen Dettmar,Ziyao Fang,Qiuyang Fu,Matthias Hoeft,Jiawei Jin,Lars Künkel,Jörn Künsemöller,Caisong Liu,Lingqi Meng,Xueli Miao,Jiarui Niu,Rukiya Rejep,Dominik J. Schwarz,Golam M. Shaifullah,Caterina Tiburzi,Christian Vocks,Olaf Wucknitz,Mengyao Xue,Mao Yuan,Youling Yue,Chunfeng Zhang,Zhen Zhang*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 利用LOFAR望远镜对PSR J0814+7429进行10年星际闪烁监测，发现闪烁时标存在明显的周年变化，通过建模确定散射屏位于距离地球0.23±0.02 kpc处，可能对应本地泡边界。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究明亮近邻脉冲星PSR J0814+7429的长期星际闪烁变化，以探测电离星际介质的小尺度结构和脉冲星系统特性。星际闪烁是研究湍流星际等离子体电子密度涨落的重要工具。

Method: 使用LOFAR高频天线在120-170 MHz频段进行2013年9月至2023年9月的长期监测。从动态谱的二维自协方差函数中提取基本闪烁参数：闪烁带宽(Δν_d)和闪烁时标(Δτ_d)。通过建模闪烁速度的周年变化来确定散射屏特性。

Result: PSR J0814+7429的闪烁时标Δτ_d时间序列显示出强烈的周年变化。通过闪烁速度周年变化的建模，确定散射屏是各向异性的，位于距离地球0.23±0.02 kpc处，很可能对应本地泡的边界。

Conclusion: 长期闪烁监测成功揭示了PSR J0814+7429的散射屏特性，散射屏位于本地泡边界附近，这为理解电离星际介质的小尺度结构和脉冲星系统的几何特性提供了重要信息。

Abstract: The interstellar scintillation observed in radio pulsars arises from interference between electromagnetic waves scattered by electron density fluctuations in the turbulent interstellar plasma, providing a critical tool for probing the small-scale structure of the ionized interstellar medium and the pulsar system itself. The primary aim of this work is to study long-term scintillation variations for a bright and nearby pulsar, PSR J0814$+$7429, carried out from 2013 September to 2023 September with the LOw-Frequency ARray (LOFAR) High Band Antennae in the frequency range of 120 - 170 MHz. We derive the basic scintillation parameters, scintillation bandwidth ($Δν_{\rm d}$) and scintillation timescale ($Δτ_{\rm d}$), from the two-dimensional (2D) auto-covariance function of the dynamic spectra that are a 2D matrix of pulse intensity as a function of time and frequency. We present the long-term monitoring of $Δν_{\rm d}$ and $Δτ_{\rm d}$ for PSR J0814$+7429$, which shows a strong annual variation in the time series of the $Δτ_{\rm d}$. From our modeling of the annual variations of scintillation velocities, the scattering screen is anisotropic and located at $0.23\pm0.02$ kpc from the Earth, likely corresponding to the boundary of the Local Bubble.

### [3] [What Are Pulsar Companions Made of? Using Gravitational Tides to Probe Their Compositions](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02725)
*Liam Colombo-Murphy,Lucas Brown,Stefano Profumo,M. Grant Roberts,Aya Westerling*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 通过分析四个脉冲星伴星的潮汐特性（拱线运动常数、轨道进动、潮汐形变度），限制其内部化学成分和结构组成，以揭示这些奇特天体的形成历史。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 低偏心率、短轨道周期的脉冲星伴星可能具有新颖致密且稳定的系外行星内部结构，其经历的显著轨道演化由强引力潮汐引起。研究这些天体的内部组成有助于理解其独特的形成历史。

Method: 使用多种状态方程模拟脉冲星伴星的潮汐特性（包括拱线运动常数、轨道进动和潮汐形变度），并与脉冲星计时观测恢复的四个系统（PSR J1719-1438b、PSR J0636+5128b、PSR J2322+2650b、PSR J1807-2459A b）数值进行比较。

Result: 通过比较模拟的潮汐特性与观测数据，希望对这些天体的化学和结构组成施加严格限制，从而约束其内部成分。

Conclusion: 限制脉冲星伴星的内部组成有助于阐明其独特的历史和形成机制，为研究致密稳定系外行星内部结构提供新途径。

Abstract: Low eccentricity, short orbital period pulsar companions may provide a probe to study novel dense and stable exoplanet internal compositions due to the potentially significant orbital evolution they experience caused by strong gravitational tides. We model the tidal characteristics such as apsidal motion constants, orbital precession, and tidal deformability for a variety of equations of state to be compared with values recovered via pulsar timing for a sample of four systems: PSR J1719-1438b, PSR J0636+5128b, PSR J2322+2650b, and PSR J1807-2459A b. With this method, we hope to place stringent limits on the chemical and structural composition of these objects. Through limiting the internal composition of pulsar companions, we aim to elucidate their unique history and formation.

### [4] [Spin effects in superfluidity, neutron matter and neutron stars](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02782)
*Armen Sedrakian,Peter B. Rau*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 综述了致密星内部物理，重点关注自旋、磁场和核子超流/超导的微观与宏观表现，讨论了核物质状态方程、多信使约束、磁效应、涡旋结构以及可能的夸克相。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 致密星（如中子星）的内部物理涉及极端条件下的强相互作用、量子效应和磁场，这些因素共同决定了星体的结构、演化和观测特征。需要系统理解自旋统计、核物质状态方程、超流超导相以及磁场的微观与宏观效应。

Method: 采用基于核能密度在对称饱和密度附近展开的元模型框架，分析状态方程各项对致密星可观测量（质量、半径、转动惯量）的影响；结合多信使观测约束；探讨磁场对致密物质和超流相的影响；研究涡旋和磁通量管晶格、钉扎与相互摩擦等介观过程。

Result: 量子简并压决定了中子星的稳定性与整体性质；强磁场对结构影响需极强场，但对超流相影响阈值较低；涡旋/磁通量管晶格及其钉扎与摩擦过程在中子星转动动力学中起核心作用；多信使观测为质量、半径、转动惯量提供了约束。

Conclusion: 致密星内部物理是强相互作用、量子统计与磁效应交织的复杂系统。涡旋结构、磁通量管构型、脉冲星自转突跳及其弛豫的起源仍是未解问题。未来需进一步探索可能的退禁闭夸克相及其色超导性质、涡旋结构与磁场响应。

Abstract: We review selected aspects of the interior physics of compact stars, focusing on the microscopic and macroscopic manifestations of spin, magnetic fields, and nucleonic superfluidity and superconductivity. Spin statistics of fermions allows quantum degeneracy pressure to determine the stability and global properties of neutron stars, whose structure depends sensitively on the strong interactions among baryons in dense matter. Using a generic meta-modeling framework based on an expansion of the nuclear energy density around the isospin-symmetric and saturation-density limits, we highlight how various lesser-known terms in this expansion affect compact-star observables and review multimessenger constraints on mass, radius, and moment of inertia. The influence of magnetic fields on dense matter is examined, showing that substantial effects in their structure require extremely strong fields, whereas lower fields are sufficient to affect their superfluid phases. At the mesoscopic scale, the coexistence of superfluid and superconducting components features vortex and flux-tube lattices, with pinning and mutual friction processes playing central roles in neutron-star rotational dynamics. We discuss unresolved issues concerning vortex structure, flux-tube configurations, and the origin of pulsar glitches and post-glitch relaxation. We also briefly address the possible emergence of deconfined quark phases in compact-star cores, including their color-superconducting properties, as well as the associated vortex structures and magnetic-field responses in such phases.

### [5] [Optical observations and atomic environment of supernova remnant G25.1-2.3](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02902)
*Ebru Aktekin,Hicran Bakış,Volkan Bakış,Yuya Asano,Hidetoshi Sano,Yasuo Fukui,Aytap Sezer*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 对超新星遗迹G25.1-2.3的多波段研究，首次分析其光学光谱数据，发现冲击加热气体和光致电离区域，并新发现HI空洞结构。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: G25.1-2.3是在中德λ6厘米银河系巡天中发现的射电超新星遗迹，需要多波段观测来深入了解其特性和环境。

Method: 使用LAMOST和RTT150望远镜的光谱数据分析[SII]/Hα等发射线比值，结合T100望远镜的Hα成像观测，以及HI和CO数据，并利用表面亮度-直径关系和能量均分方法分析演化阶段。

Result: 首次获得光学光谱数据，[SII]/Hα比值在0.16-0.83之间变化；识别出北部和南部的冲击加热气体及多个光致电离区域；电子密度北部120-1030 cm⁻³，南部490-4500 cm⁻³；Hα/Hβ比值显示显著消光差异；新发现与SNR直径一致的HI空洞结构。

Conclusion: 多波段研究揭示了G25.1-2.3的复杂物理特性和环境，光学光谱数据提供了新见解，HI空洞的发现为理解SNR与星际介质相互作用提供了重要线索。

Abstract: The supernova remnant (SNR) G25.1-2.3 was identified in the radio band during the Sino-German $λ$6 cm survey of the Galactic plane. We present a detailed investigation of the optical, HI, and CO emission towards the G25.1-2.3 to better understand its characteristics and environment. In this study, optical spectroscopic data of the remnant and its environment have been analysed for the first time, providing new insights into their emission properties. The Large Sky Area Multi-Object Fiber Spectroscopic Telescope (LAMOST) and 1.5-m Russian-Turkish Telescope (RTT150) data show variations across the observed regions, with [SII]/H$α$ ranging from 0.16 to 0.83. We identified shock-heated gas in the northern and southern regions and several photoionized regions around the SNR based on their [SII]/H$α$ ratios derived from spectra. The [SII]$λ$6716/$λ$6731 ratio observed in the northern region suggests electron densities ($n_{\rm e}$) ranging from 120 to 1030 cm$^{-3}$, whereas the southern regions show higher values, between 490 and 4500 cm$^{-3}$. The variations in the observed H$α$/H$β$ ratios indicate significant differences in extinction across the regions. H$α$ images obtained using the 1-m Turkish Telescope (T100) reveal optical emission in the northern and southern, characterized by filamentary and diffuse structures. We newly found a hole-like distribution of HI, whose spatial extent is roughly consistent with the diameter of the SNR. Based on radio data, we examine the evolutionary stage of G25.1-2.3 using the surface brightness-diameter ($Σ-D$) relation and the equipartition method.

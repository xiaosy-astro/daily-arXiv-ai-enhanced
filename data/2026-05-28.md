### [1] [Multi-mission Observations of GS 1354-64 during the 2025-26 Outburst: First Results](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.27415)
*Dipak Debnath,Hsiang-Kuang Chang,Subham Srimani,Anuj Nandi*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 本文报道了银河系暂现黑洞候选体GS 1354-64在2025-26年爆发的新观测结果，利用MAXI、NuSTAR和Insight-HXMT多卫星数据，分析了其奇特的光变曲线、能谱和准周期振荡（QPO）特征，并估算了黑洞自旋和倾角。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: GS 1354-64是一次罕见的黑洞暂现源爆发事件，其光变呈现不寻常的短时标X射线耀发（~3天）且HID演化路径不遵循典型正反循环模式，值得深入探测其物理机制。

Method: 利用MAXI、NuSTAR和Insight-HXMT的宽能段（2-70 keV）观测数据进行能谱（diskbb+relxill模型）和时间分析；利用传播振荡激波模型研究低频QPO的频率演化（0.07-4 Hz）。

Result: 爆发呈现快速上升与双耀发结构（峰值~1.4 Crab和~0.8 Crab）；HID演化不遵循标准回环；低频QPO频率单调上升/下降；能谱显著包含相对论性反射成分；自旋a_k~0.998，倾角i~63°-70°。

Conclusion: 该源具有极端自旋和高倾角，QPO行为可用激波传播模型解释；其不寻常的HID演化路径和耀发结构表明该爆发与典型黑洞暂现源行为存在差异，可能受吸积流性质或盘结构影响。

Abstract: The Galactic transient black hole GS 1354-64 recently showed a new outburst, which has been continuously monitored by {\it MAXI}, {\it NuSTAR} and {\it Insight-HXMT} missions. The ongoing $2025-26$ outburst shows a slow-rise with an unusual short period ($\sim 3$~days) of X-ray flare of peak flux $\sim 1.4$~Crab, followed by another relatively weak flare of intensity $\sim 0.8$ Crab. The source is observed to evolve through `canonical' spectral states in a Hardness-Intensity Diagram (HID) during rising phase of the outburst, however subsequent outburst profile did not follow the reverse trend of the HID. A rapid evolution of Quasi-periodic Oscillation (QPO) frequencies ($\sim 0.07-4$ Hz) is observed during hard/intermediate spectral states without any signature of QPOs in the soft state. The evolution of the observed low frequency QPOs shows a monotonically increasing (rising phase) as well as decreasing (decay phase) signature, which are further studied with the propagating oscillatory shock model to understand the nature of the evolution of the shock wave, responsible for the origin of the observed QPOs. The broadband energy spectra from {\it NuSTAR} ($3-70$ keV) and {\it Insight-HXMT} ($2-60$ keV) are well described by thermal (\textit{diskbb}) and reflection (\textit{relxill}) model components, indicating a strong signature of a relativistic reflection feature. Using `canonical' softer state observations, we found the source to be maximally rotating with $a_k \sim 0.998$ and inclination angle to be as $i \sim 63^\circ - 70^\circ$, which are consistent with earlier reports.

### [2] [The strength of Type-C quasi-periodic oscillations in black hole X-ray binaries correlates with the jet inclination](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.27510)
*F. M. Vincentelli,N. Bollemeijer,A. Veledina,D. Altamirano,Q. Bu,F. Carotenuto,P. Casella,Y. Cavecchi,R. Ma,G. Marcel,G. Mastroserio,S. Motta,L. Zhang*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 本文首次定量量化了低质量X射线双星中黑洞的Type-C准周期振荡（QPO）振幅与喷流倾角之间的关系，发现存在显著的线性相关性（最高至8 Hz），支持QPO的“几何”起源假说。同时发现爆发衰减期的QPO振幅系统性地低于上升期，并与旋进热流模型的预测进行比较，要求自旋-轨道错位角至少为10-15°。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 尽管已知低质量X射线双星中黑洞QPO振幅与倾角有关，但实际依赖关系从未被充分定量估计。本文旨在利用改进的倾角测量和HXMT卫星数据，首次量化这种关系。

Method: 利用RXTE档案数据和HXMT卫星最新观测，结合射电观测改进的喷流倾角测量，分析单个黑洞低质量X射线双星中Type-C QPO振幅与倾角的关系，并与旋进热流模型预测进行比较。

Result: 发现Type-C QPO振幅与喷流倾角之间存在显著的线性相关性（最高8 Hz）；爆发衰减期振幅系统低于上升期；旋进热流模型要求自旋-轨道错位角至少为10-15°才能复现观测振幅。

Conclusion: QPO的“几何”起源假说得到加强；该数据集为任何QPO模型提供了关键基准。

Abstract: X-ray quasi-periodic oscillations (QPOs) are a characteristic feature of low-mass X-ray binaries (LMXBs). These oscillations have been studied for decades and revealed a rich and complex phenomenology that is still not fully understood. RXTE archival studies have shown that the amplitude of these oscillations differs significantly between black holes (BH) with high or low inclination. Yet, the actual dependence on inclination has never been adequately estimated. Thanks to the improvement of inclination measurements through radio observations and the recent observations by the HXMT satellite, we quantified for the first time the dependence of Type-C QPO amplitudes on the jet inclination of individual BH LMXBs. Our analysis reveals the presence of a significant linear correlation up to 8 Hz, strengthening the case for a ''geometrical'' origin of the QPOs. In addition, for a given QPO frequency, we observe systematically lower amplitudes during the decay of outbursts compared to the rise. This data collection represents a key benchmark for any QPO model. Our comparison with the predictions from a precessing hot flow shows that the amplitude of the QPOs can be reproduced by this scenario if the spin-orbit misalignment is at least $\approx$10-15$^\circ$.

### [3] [Fermi-LAT View on Three Ultra-high-energy 1LHAASO Sources in the $52^{\circ}<l<55^{\circ}$ Region](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.28081)
*Linjie Liu,Xian Hou,Pierrick Martin,Chuyuan Yang*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 利用17年Fermi-LAT数据，将银河系$52°<l<55°$区域的伽马射线辐射解析为3个点源和2个延展源，研究了它们与三个LHAASO超高清源及已知脉冲星、超新星遗迹的关联，发现能谱和形态上存在不匹配，需要更深层次观测和建模。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 该区域包含三个LHAASO探测到的超高能伽马射线源，且存在多个超新星遗迹、脉冲星和GeV/TeV源，但它们在GeV波段的详细辐射性质和物理起源尚不清楚，需要高分辨率分析来厘清源之间的关联。

Method: 利用超过17年的Fermi-LAT数据，对该区域进行详细的GeV伽马射线分析，通过空间和能谱建模将辐射分解为点源和延展源，并与已知的脉冲星、超新星遗迹、PWN以及LHAASO/HAWC的TeV观测进行交叉比对。

Result: 解析出J1925+1729P（新脉冲星候选体）、J1930+1851P（对应G54.1+0.3，可能为轻子起源）、J1932+1916P（确认脉冲星）、J1929+1732E（混合轻子-强子模型）和J1930+1826E（强子模型）五个GeV源，但与三个LHAASO超高能源存在能谱和/或形态不匹配。

Conclusion: 该区域的GeV与TeV辐射起源复杂，现有机理尚不能完美统一，需要HAWC/LHAASO进一步观测、改进银盘弥散模型以及未来CTAO高角分辨率观测来厘清源的本质和关联。

Abstract: Using more than 17 yr of Fermi-LAT data, we performed a detailed investigation of the complex $52^{\circ}<l<55^{\circ}$ region, which encompasses the three ultra-high-energy sources 1LHAASO J1928+1746u, 1LHAASO J1928+1813u, and 1LHAASO J1929+1846u.This region hosts multiple SNRs, pulsars, GeV and TeV sources. Our analysis resolves the GeV emission into three pointlike sources (J1925+1729P, J1930+1851P, and J1932+1916P) and two extended sources (J1929+1732E and J1930+1826E), and improves significantly on the description based on the 4FGL-DR4 catalog. Source J1932+1916P is identified as the known gamma-ray pulsar PSR J1932+1916, while J1925+1729P may be a new gamma-ray pulsar candidate distinct from the known gamma-ray pulsar PSR J1925+1720. This warrants future investigation and a search for pulsations. Source J1930+1851P coincides with the TeV source PWN/SNR G54.1+0.3 and its GeV-TeV spectrum is consistent with both leptonic and hadronic interpretations, although a leptonic origin in relation to the known PWN is more likely. The GeV-TeV spectrum of J1929+1732E is consistent with a hybrid lepto-hadronic scenario in which the TeV emission traces the PWN powered by the pulsar PSR J1928+1746, while the GeV emission may result from interactions between particles escaped from the parent SNR and illuminating the gas environment. Similarly, J1930+1826E is likely connected to PWN/SNR G54.1+0.3 under a hadronic scenario involving escaped particles in their early propagation stage. Owing to spectral and/or morphological mismatches, the connection of these five GeV sources to the three LHAASO sources is not clear. This warrants deeper observations with HAWC and LHAASO, and a dedicated study of the modeling of the Galactic diffuse emission. Future CTAO observations with higher angular resolution are expected to deliver crucial information for the study of this region.

</details>

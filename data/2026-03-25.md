Motivation: 研究微类星体（如SS 433）作为宇宙射线加速源的可能性，特别是它们能否贡献到宇宙射线能谱的膝区（约4 PeV）。SS 433位于W50超新星遗迹内，其相对论性喷流与周围介质相互作用，为研究喷流驱动的粒子加速提供了理想案例。

Method: 使用VERITAS望远镜进行超过150小时的深度观测，能量范围>100 GeV，角分辨率优于0.1度。进行形态学和光谱学研究，包括相位分辨分析（轨道相位和进动相位），以检测辐射的变异性。

Result: 在东西喷流瓣（距离中央双星数十秒差距）都探测到延展的TeV伽马射线辐射，辐射沿喷流轴方向延伸，与喷流和超新星遗迹相互作用的区域重合。中央双星未探测到TeV辐射，中央与喷流瓣之间也无显著辐射。相位分析显示无轨道或进动相位相关的变异性，支持稳态辐射模型。光谱和形态特征符合粒子在喷流瓣中通过激波或磁重联等过程加速的场景。

Conclusion: SS 433喷流瓣的延展TeV辐射在VERITAS能区倾向于轻子起源，任何强子加速过程都处于次要地位。这些结果为微类星体喷流如何贡献银河系宇宙射线种群（特别是向膝区方向）提供了重要约束。

Abstract: Microquasars such as SS 433 are considered potential contributors to cosmic rays up to the knee of the cosmic ray energy spectrum ($\sim4\,\mathrm{PeV}$), where a transition in the dominant acceleration processes is expected. The SS 433 system, located within the W50 supernova remnant, is a Galactic microquasar with relativistic jets interacting with the surrounding medium over parsec scales, providing an example for studying jet-driven particle acceleration. A deep morphological and spectral study of SS 433 is performed using more than 150 hours of observations with VERITAS, sensitive to $γ$-ray energies $>100\,\mathrm{GeV}$. With an angular resolution better than $0.1^°$, extended TeV $γ$-ray emission is resolved from both the eastern and western jet lobes, located tens of parsecs from the central binary. The emission appears elongated along the jet axis and coincides with regions where the jets interact with the surrounding supernova remnant. No TeV emission is detected from the central binary, nor is significant emission observed between the central binary and the jet lobes. Phase-resolved analyses show no evidence for variability with orbital or precessional phase, supporting a steady emission scenario. The observed morphology and spectra are consistent with scenarios where particles are accelerated in the lobes of the jets, possibly through shocks or alternative processes such as magnetic reconnection. The extended TeV emission from the jet lobes of SS 433 favors a leptonic origin in the VERITAS energy range, suggesting any hadronic acceleration is subdominant. The results offer valuable constraints on how microquasar jets may contribute to the Galactic cosmic-ray population toward the knee.

### [32] [Characterizing Short-Timescale Optical Variability in Non-blazar Active Galactic Nucleus PKS~0521$-$36 Using TESS](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.22197)
*Sikandar Akbar,Zahir Shah*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 利用TESS卫星对非耀变体AGN PKS 0521-36进行高时间分辨率光学光变曲线分析，首次在具有直接成像螺旋喷流的非耀变体AGN中探测到光学准周期振荡(QPO)。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究非耀变体活动星系核的光学变异性特征，探索喷流中的物理过程，特别是寻找准周期振荡信号以理解喷流动力学机制。

Method: 使用TESS卫星在三个观测扇区（Sector 5、6、32）获取高时间分辨率光学光变曲线，采用功率谱密度分析、Lomb-Scargle周期图、小波变换(WWZ)和阻尼随机游走(DRW)模型等多种统计方法进行变异性分析。

Result: 探测到显著的光学准周期振荡(P=2.838±0.078天，置信度>99.99%)，功率谱密度呈现弯曲幂律特征，光变分布需要双对数正态模型描述，表明存在两种不同的光学通量状态。QPO信号仅在Sector 5中检测到，具有瞬态特征。

Conclusion: 观测到的光学QPO可能是相对论性喷流中磁流体动力学扭折不稳定性引起的，与观测到的螺旋结构一致。结合先前报告的伽马射线QPO，表明存在层次化的变异性结构，为喷流物理提供了新的观测证据。

Abstract: We present a systematic analysis of high-cadence optical light curves of the non-blazar AGN PKS~0521$-$36 obtained with \textit{TESS} across three sectors: Sectors~5 and~6 (Cycle~1, 30~min cadence) and Sector~32 (Cycle~3, 10~min cadence). The source exhibits moderate variability with $F_\mathrm{var} \approx 0.69$--$1.19\%$, consistent with a mildly beamed jet. The power spectral density (PSD) in all sectors is better described by a bending power-law than a simple power law, with high-frequency slopes $α_1 \approx 2.1$--2.9, indicating red-noise dominated variability. Flux distributions require two-component models, with a double log-normal providing the best description, suggesting the presence of two distinct optical flux states associated with quiescent jet emission and episodic flaring activity. A significant QPO at $P = 2.838 \pm 0.078$~d is detected in Sector~5 at $>99.99\%$ confidence in the Lomb--Scargle periodogram, independently confirmed by WWZ ($2.839 \pm 0.110$~d) and supported at the $3σ$ level by DRW analysis. The signal spans $\sim$9 cycles within the 26.1-day baseline in Sector~5 and is absent in Sectors~6 and~32, indicating a transient feature. The PSD bending frequency ($ν_b \approx 0.308$~d$^{-1}$; $\sim$3.2~d) is consistent with the QPO period, suggesting a common origin. We interpret the oscillation as magnetohydrodynamic kink instabilities in the relativistic jet, consistent with the observed helicoidal structure. A moderate Doppler factor ($δ\approx 5$--10) naturally explains the day-scale periodicity. Together with previously reported $γ$-ray QPOs on longer timescales, this suggests a hierarchical variability structure, and, to the best of our knowledge, provides the first evidence for an optical QPO in a non-blazar AGN with a directly imaged helical jet.

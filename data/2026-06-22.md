### [1] [Nonlinear Decay of Fast Magnetosonic Waves through Weak Turbulence: Force-Free Electrodynamics Simulations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.19450)
*Siddhant Solanki,Jens Mahlmann,Alexander Philippov*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 本文通过相对论性无力电动力学模拟，研究强磁化环境中低频快磁声波的非线性演化，发现参量衰变不稳定性会导致FMS波高效转化为次级FMS波和Alfvén波，使得波能持续耗散且频谱显著展宽，最终表明FMS波难以从磁星磁层中无实质损耗地逃逸。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探讨GHz快速射电暴（FRB）是否能在磁星内磁层产生后，以低频快磁声波（FMS）的形式逃逸而不经历显著的能量再处理。

Method: 采用相对论性无力电动力学模拟，验证Golbraikh & Lyubarsky (2023)的理论预测，分析参量衰变不稳定性对FMS波的转换过程。

Result: FMS波通过参量衰变不稳定性高效转换为次级FMS波和Alfvén波；即使在FMS与Alfvén分量达到近似能量均分后，能量耗散仍在继续；激发波谱展宽至模拟域惯性范围的大部分k空间。

Conclusion: 低频快磁声波难以从磁星磁层中无显著耗散和频谱展宽地逃逸，这对FRB的起源机制提出了重要约束。

Abstract: We investigate the propagation of low-frequency fast-magnetosonic (FMS) waves in highly magnetized environments. Such conditions are relevant to the escape of GHz fast radio bursts potentially produced in the inner magnetospheres of magnetars. It remains an open question whether such waves can escape without substantial reprocessing. Using relativistic force-free electrodynamics simulations, we confirm the key theoretical predictions of Golbraikh & Lyubarsky (2023) and demonstrate that FMS waves undergo efficient nonlinear conversion into secondary FMS and Alfvén waves via the parametric decay instability. This process continues to drain energy from the primary FMS waves even after approximate energy equipartition between the FMS and Alfvén components is established. The resulting spectrum of excited waves is broad, extending across much of the inertial range in $k$-space within the simulation domain. Our results indicate that FMS waves likely do not escape magnetar magnetospheres without substantial dissipation and spectral broadening.

### [2] [Evidence for candidate X-ray pulsations from the ultraluminous X-ray source NGC 7456 ULX-1](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.20308)
*Yuanle Yao,Xiang-Dong Li,Xiao-Jie Xu*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 在NGC7456 ULX-1（一个超亮X射线源）中发现了~0.22 Hz的候选脉动信号，暗示其可能包含一个吸积中子星，表面偶极磁场约10^12-10^14 G，但需进一步观测确认。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 确定NGC7456 ULX-1的致密天体性质（中子星还是黑洞），以理解超亮X射线源的物理本质。

Method: 利用2023年XMM-Newton观测数据，采用加速搜索、Z_n^2统计量、轨道解调分析等多种独立计时技术识别脉动信号。

Result: 探测到~0.22 Hz的候选脉动信号，其频率在观测期间漂移，并估算表面偶极磁场强度为10^12-10^14 G。

Conclusion: NGC7456 ULX-1可能包含一个吸积中子星，但需要独立数据或更多观测来确认。

Abstract: We report evidence for a candidate pulsational signal at $\sim0.22$~Hz from NGC7456 ULX-1, a previously identified ultraluminous X-ray source (ULX). The signal is identified in the 2023 XMM-Newton observation using independent timing techniques including accelerated searches, $Z^2_n$ statistics, and an orbital-demodulation analysis designed to restore phase coherence in the presence of binary motion. The candidate pulsation frequency drift within the observation suggests rapid spin evolution driven by accretion torque. We further estimate the surface dipole magnetic field strength to be $B\sim 10^{12}-10^{14}$ G. These results provide evidence that NGC7456 ULX-1 may host an accreting neutron star, although confirmation with independent datasets or additional observations is required.

</details>

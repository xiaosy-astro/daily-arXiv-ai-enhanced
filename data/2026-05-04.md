TL;DR: 本文利用144 ks深度钱德拉X射线观测数据，分析了星系团RXJ2014.8-2430中的冷锋结构，确认了三个冷锋的位置，并发现了一个可能由Kelvin-Helmholtz不稳定性或AGN活动引起的大型凹状结构。冷锋宽度测量表明扩散被抑制，而凹状特征若为气泡，则其能量与最强观测气泡相当。

Motivation: 前期XMM-Newton和浅层Chandra数据已显示该星系团存在大尺度冷锋和两个较年轻的冷锋，但缺乏高分辨率数据以分析这些冷锋的精细小尺度结构。

Method: 利用144 ks深度Chandra观测数据，结合beta模型减除和高斯梯度幅度滤波两种方法，分析冷锋的精细结构，测量冷锋宽度，并评估凹状结构的物理性质。

Abstract: We analyze our new 144 ks deep Chandra observation of the sloshing cold front cluster RXJ2014.8-2430. Previous observations of RXJ2014.8-2430 with XMM-Newton shows evidence of a large scale, sloshing cold front around 800 kpc away from the cluster core. Previous shallow Chandra data also shows evidence of two younger cold fronts closer to the core. Our new deeper Chandra data allow us to analyze the fine, small scale structure of these three cold fronts. Using both beta model subtraction and Gaussian Gradient Magnitude filtering, we confirm the locations of the three cold fronts, as well as discover a large concave structure southeast of the cluster core near the outermost cold front, which could be a large Kelvin-Helmholtz instability or a gas cavity from AGN activity. Analyzing the three cold fronts, we measure the widths of the cold fronts and find them to be consistent with or lower than the Coulomb mean free paths within error, signifying that diffusion is suppressed across the cold fronts. If the concave feature is the inner rim of a cavity, we find that it has a radius in the range 200-330kpc, and would have $PV$ values in the range of $5.7 \times 10^{60}$ - $2.7 \times 10^{61}$ erg. These values would make it consistent with the some of the most powerful bubbles observed.

### [2] [Random Polarization Position Angle Behaviors across Bursts of Repeating Fast Radio Bursts](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.00372)

TL;DR: 本文发现重复快速射电暴（FRB）的偏振位置角（PA）近似呈高斯分布，表明辐射源于中子星磁层局部区域；通过扩展旋转矢量模型引入动态演化的磁层，解释PA分布及无周期性的观测特征。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 理解FRB偏振位置角（PA）的统计特性及其物理起源，解释不同爆发间PA变化但统计特征稳定的现象，并探寻可能的周期性信号。

Method: 分析重复FRB的PA分布统计特性，使用Lomb-Scargle周期图在10 ms到10^7 ms范围内搜索周期性信号；在理论上扩展旋转矢量模型，引入随机扰动导致的动态演化磁层。

Result: 重复FRB的固有PA呈近似高斯分布；在宽周期范围内未检测到可信的周期信号；多个活跃观测数据同样无周期信号发现。

Abstract: Fast radio bursts (FRBs), highly polarized, mostly have a nearly constant polarization position angle (PA) during each burst. Their PAs are observed to vary from burst to burst, with the statistical properties remaining stable across different observation sessions. We found that the intrinsic PAs of repeating FRBs are approximately Gaussian distributed, suggesting that the emission likely originates from a localized region within the neutron star's magnetosphere. A periodicity search of the PA time series using the Lomb-Scargle periodogram reveals no credible periodic signal in the period range from 10 ms to $10^7$ ms, and similar analyses of several active observations also yield null detections. We interpret these properties by extending the rotating vector model to include a dynamically evolving magnetosphere, in which the effective magnetic axis varies from burst to burst due to stochastic perturbations. In this framework, the observed PA distributions can naturally arise from geometric projection effects, and the absence of periodicity reflects the random wandering of the magnetic axis within a confined region. This scenario provides a natural explanation for both repeating and apparently non-repeating FRBs.

### [3] [Maximal mass of neutron stars constrained by neutron star observations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.00437)
*Gábor Kasza,György Wolf*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 本论文通过贝叶斯加权框架，结合多信使观测约束（GW170817、NICER等），研究中子星物质高密度状态方程，发现最大质量分布集中在2.2-2.3 M☉，对应半径约12±1 km，且端点分布对约束高密度EOS行为具有敏感性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探究高密度中子星物质的状态方程，利用多信使观测数据在贝叶斯框架下约束M(R)序列端点（最大质量M_TOV及其对应半径R_TOV）的概率分布。

Method: 以SFHo和DD2两种强子基线EOS为基础，在高密度区域匹配扩展线性sigma模型描述，并受到pQCD结果的约束，构造因果混合EOS家族。结合GW170817、NICER质量-半径测量及候选低质量和质量间隙致密天体的观测约束，通过贝叶斯似然加权确定概率分布。

Result: 最大质量分布主要受观测约束决定，对基线EOS选择不敏感，集中在2.2-2.3 M☉；对应半径分布则强烈依赖于强子EOS，典型值约12±1 km。潮汐形变约束与质量间隙中子星候选体共同排除了过刚的EOS。

Conclusion: M(R)序列端点分布是在多信使贝叶斯框架下约束中子星EOS高密度行为的灵敏且互补的诊断工具。

Abstract: We investigate constraints on the high-density equation of state (EOS) of neutron star matter by analyzing the probability distributions of the endpoints of mass-radius M(R) sequences within a Bayesian weighting framework. Starting from two representative hadronic baseline EOSs, SFHo and DD2, matched at higher densities to an extended linear sigma model description and constrained to approach perturbative QCD (pQCD) results, we construct families of causal hybrid EOSs spanning a broad range of stiffness at supranuclear densities. Observational constraints from the binary neutron-star merger GW170817, mass-radius measurements from the Neutron Star Interior Composition Explorer (NICER), and candidate low-mass and mass-gap compact objects are incorporated through Bayesian likelihood weighting. This approach allows us to determine probability distributions for the maximum neutron-star mass M$_{\rm TOV}$ and the corresponding radius R$_{\rm TOV}$, i.e., the endpoints of the M(R) sequences.
  We find that the maximum-mass distributions are largely determined by observational constraints and show only weak sensitivity to the choice of baseline EOS, favoring values around 2.2-2.3 M$_\odot$ when the most robust constraints are applied. In contrast, the corresponding radius distributions exhibit a stronger dependence on the underlying hadronic EOS, with typical preferred values near $12\pm 1$ km. Additional tidal-deformability constraints further restrict the allowed parameter space and disfavor very stiff EOS realizations when interpreted together with the possible mass-gap neutron-star candidate. Our results demonstrate that endpoint distributions of M(R) sequences provide a sensitive and complementary diagnostic for constraining the high-density behavior of the neutron-star EOS within a multimessenger Bayesian framework.

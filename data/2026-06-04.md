### [1] [Temporal Invariance Is an Illusion: Time-Dependent Influences of the Galactic Magnetic Field on UHECR Observations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.04140)
*Veronika Vašíčková,Leonel Morejón,Karl-Heinz Kampert*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 本研究通过CRPropa 3.2模拟发现，银河系磁场对超高能宇宙射线（UHECRs）造成的数百千年延迟会显著改变其能谱、成分和到达方向，在瞬态爆发场景下刚性10^18-10^19 V范围内出现能谱截断，为UHECR光谱截断提供了不依赖源加速极限的替代解释。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有模型忽略了UHECR可观测量的时间演化，特别是在爆发性源场景中这一因素尤为重要。本研究旨在探索银河系磁场引起的时间延迟对地球观测到的UHECR能谱和到达方向的影响。

Method: 使用CRPropa 3.2模拟银河系磁场中河外宇宙射线的驻留时间，研究其刚性依赖性，并分析时间延迟对UHECR可观测量的影响。

Result: UHECR进入银河系后可经历数百千年的延迟；瞬态场景下刚性10^18-10^19 V范围内出现与观测数据吻合的能谱截断；成分逐渐向重核偏移；延迟分布与银河系磁场强度相关；约100千年尺度上出现偶极各向异性。

Conclusion: 银河系磁场延迟效应为UHECR光谱截断提供了不依赖源加速极限的替代解释，可能修正现有约束条件。

Abstract: Understanding the origin of the Ultra-High-Energy Cosmic Rays (UHECRs) requires explaining the features of their energy spectrum, mass composition, and arrival directions. Current modeling approaches neglect the time evolution of UHECR observables, a factor that is particularly important in the case of bursting UHECR sources. This study focuses on the influence of time delays caused by the galactic magnetic field (GMF) on the spectrum and arrival directions of UHECRs observed on Earth. Using CRPropa 3.2, we investigate the rigidity-dependence of the residence time of extragalactic cosmic rays entering our Galaxy. We find that UHECRs entering the Milky Way can experience delays of hundreds of kiloyears relative to light, and we demonstrate that these delays significantly alter the UHECR observables. Notably, a cutoff emerges in the transient scenario within the rigidity range of $10^{18}-10^{19}$ V, which coincides with the spectral break observed in data. We find a progressive shift in composition favoring heavier nuclei, as well as a delay distribution that is correlated with GMF strength. This causes the particles to be less correlated with their initial direction the larger their delays. A dipole-like anisotropy develops over timescales of about $\sim$100 kyr in certain bursts scenarios. Our results provide an alternative explanation for the UHECR spectral cutoff that does not invoke limits on source acceleration. This could potentially revise existing constraints.

### [2] [Periodic Radio and X-ray Emission from an Accreting White Dwarf Binary](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.04232)
*Kovi Rose,Joshua Pritchard,Tara Murphy,L. N. Driessen,D. L. Kaplan,M. Caleb,Ziteng Wang,A. Zic,I. Andreoni,J. Carney,B. N. Barlow,D. Dobie,M. Gu,G. Heald,D. Huber,E. Lenc,J. K. Leung,W. Lu,R. Momose,M. G. Pedersen,Y. Qu,N. Rea,I. de Ruiter,K. Shaji,G. R. Sivakoff,A. J. M. Thomson,Y. L. Wang,G. J. Yang,F. Zahedy*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 本文报告了发现并分类长周期射电暂现源ASKAP J174508.9-505149为一个吸积白矮双星系统（激变变星），该源具有~1.3小时光谱轨道周期、轨道调制的X射线发射和射电暴，椭圆偏振的射电暴有频率漂移现象，加强了部分长周期射电暂现源与白矮双星之间的关联。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 长周期射电暂现源（LPTs）的物理起源尚不明确，虽然有慢自转磁星和白矮星-伴星快速轨道运动两种假说，但产生明亮射电脉冲的机制仍知之甚少，需要更多观测证据来厘清其本质。

Method: 通过光谱观测确认轨道周期（~1.3小时），结合X射线和射电多波段观测，分析轨道调制的X射线发射和射电暴特征（椭圆偏振、频率漂移、间歇性熄灭），利用特征光学发射线鉴定其为吸积激变变星系统。

Result: 成功将ASKAP J174508.9-505149分类为吸积白矮双星（激变变星），确认了光谱轨道周期、轨道调制的X射线和射电辐射，发现射电暴具有椭圆偏振和频率漂移现象，且会间歇性关闭数小时。

Conclusion: 该发现加强了至少部分长周期射电暂现源与白矮双星之间的关联，表明吸积白矮双星系统可以产生此类长周期射电辐射，为理解LPTs的物理起源提供了重要线索。

Abstract: Long period radio transients (LPTs) are coherent bursts of polarised radio emission that repeat periodically on timescales of minutes to hours. Little is known about the physical origins of these systems. Astronomers have proposed magnetars that rotate slowly and white dwarfs that rapidly orbit with a companion star as potential explanations. While several recent examples appear to support the latter hypothesis, the mechanism generating these bright radio pulses remains poorly understood. Here we report our discovery and classification of the LPT ASKAP J174508.9-505149 as an accreting white dwarf binary. This object has a ~1.3h spectroscopic orbital period and exhibits orbitally-modulated X-ray emission and radio bursts. These elliptically polarised radio bursts drift in emission frequency, potentially due to a longer beat period, and turn off for several hours at a time. Some long period radio transients have been associated with non-interacting white dwarf binaries. We have spectroscopically confirmed this system as an accreting cataclysmic variable, identified through characteristic optical emission lines and an ongoing X-ray outburst. Our results strengthen the link between at least some long period radio transients and white dwarf binaries.

### [3] [Granular mass perturbations on the pulsar - supermassive black hole system](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.04762)
*Zexin Hu,Lijing Shao*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 发现围绕人马座A*的脉冲星面临恒星质量黑洞颗粒状尖峰的扰动，导致10-100秒的计时残差，即使在紧密轨道（Pb=0.5年）上也是如此。这打破了传统认知，并提出使用近星点数据和考虑光线传播中的参考系拖拽效应来改进SMBH自旋测量精度达一个数量级。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探索围绕银河系中心超大质量黑洞人马座A*的脉冲星能否提供研究黑洞时空、检验引力理论和探测天体物理环境的独特机会，同时识别并解决未知质量分布可能引起的计时残差问题。

Method: 通过大规模数值模拟，首次研究银河系中心恒星质量黑洞颗粒状尖峰对脉冲星计时的影响，分析相位连接和相位不连接假设下的数据处理策略。

Result: 发现传统观念中认为紧密轨道可忽略的扰动实际上导致10-100秒的后拟合计时残差；在相位不连接假设下，考虑光线传播的参考系拖拽效应可打破参数简并，将SMBH自旋测量精度提高一个数量级。

Conclusion: 银河系中心的恒星质量黑洞颗粒状尖峰对脉冲星计时造成严重干扰，需要采用近星点数据策略并考虑参考系拖拽效应才能有效提取SMBH参数。

Abstract: Discovery and timing observations of a radio pulsar orbiting around Sagittarius A*, the supermassive black hole (SMBH) in our Galactic Centre (GC), will provide unprecedented opportunities of studying the SMBH spacetime, testing gravity theories, and probing the astrophysical environment in the GC. However, unknown mass distributions might cause timing residuals that are much larger than the timing precision. With extensive numerical simulations, for the first time we find that the perturbations caused by a granular cusp of stellar-mass black holes in the GC lead to post-fit timing residuals of 10-100 s, contrary to traditional wisdom, even for a pulsar in a tight orbit with an orbital period $P_b=0.5\,{\rm yr}$. Such a large timing residual can lead to significant measurement bias or even prevent construction of a phase-connected timing solution for the full orbit. We revisit the idea of extracting SMBH parameters only with data around periastron where the perturbation is small. Under the realistic phase-disconnected assumption, we point out that it is vital to consider the frame-dragging effect in the light propagation, which breaks parameter degeneracy and leads to an order of magnitude improvement for the measurement precision of the SMBH spin.

### [4] [$E_{\rm peak}$-$α$ Correlation in Time Resolved GRB Spectra: A Bottom-Up Approach with Optically Thin Inverse Compton Scattering Model](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.04894)
*Pragyan Pratim Bordoloi,Ayush Shivkumar,Shabnam Iyyani*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 本文通过模拟光学薄逆康普顿散射（ICS）在标准火球喷流框架下的时间分辨光谱演化，发现ICS能产生硬到软和强度追踪两种常见光谱演化模式，且E_peak-α呈现正相关，α从硬（Planck-like, >+0.5）演化到软（<-0.67），这种特征可作为GRB瞬时辐射阶段主导机制为ICS的诊断标志。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 伽马射线暴（GRB）是宇宙中最亮的爆炸，但其辐射起源仍不确定。时间分辨光谱分析能揭示光谱形状的演化，约束辐射机制和发射区微观物理，因此需要探究何种辐射机制可以产生观测中常见的两种光谱演化模式（硬到软演化与强度追踪）。

Method: 采用自下而上的方法，在标准火ball喷流框架下模拟光学薄逆康普顿散射（ICS）产生的时间分辨光谱演化。使用贝叶斯块和常量通量分箱两种方式对光谱进行分箱，并用经验性的Band函数对观测光谱进行一致建模。

Result: 光学薄ICS产生正的E_peak-α相关性，α从硬值（Planck-like, >+0.5）演化到软值（<-0.67）。这种硬的α值与标准同步辐射不一致。该特征演化可作为光学薄ICS主导GRB瞬时辐射阶段的诊断标志。此外，单脉冲内α的平滑演化不需要调用不同辐射机制之间的转变。

Conclusion: E_peak-α平面的特征演化可作为光学薄ICS作为GRB瞬时辐射主导机制的诊断标志，而单脉冲内α的平滑演化无需假设不同辐射机制之间的切换。

Abstract: Gamma-ray bursts (GRBs) are the brightest explosions in the Universe, yet the origin of their emission remains uncertain. Time-resolved spectral analysis offers key insights into the evolution of spectral shapes, constraining both radiation mechanisms and emission-site microphysics. Observationally, GRB spectra are well described by the empirical Band function, characterized by the peak energy ($E_{\mathrm{peak}}$) and low-energy spectral index ($α$). We investigate the temporal evolution of spectra produced by optically thin inverse-Compton scattering (ICS) within a standard fireball jet framework, focusing on the scenarios that can produce the two commonly observed spectral evolution patterns: hard-to-soft evolution and intensity tracking, within a single emission pulse. The evolution is analysed using both Bayesian block and constant-fluence binning, with the observed spectrum modeled consistently using the Band function. Using this bottom-up approach, we find that optically thin ICS yields a positive $E_{\mathrm{peak}}$-$α$ correlation, with $α$ evolving from hard (Planck-like, $> +0.5$) to softer ($< -0.67$) values. Such hard $α$ values are inconsistent with standard synchrotron emission. This characteristic evolution in the $E_{\mathrm{peak}}$-$α$ plane, therefore, provides a diagnostic signature of optically thin ICS as the dominant radiation mechanism during the prompt phase of GRBs. Furthermore, this type of smooth evolution of $α$ within a single pulse does not require invoking a transition between different radiation mechanisms, unless additional observational evidence supports such a change.

</details>
